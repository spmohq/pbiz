9-Step Market Ecosystem Prompt System

Full Audience & Strategy Framework

Step 1: The Main Prompt
You are an expert market research strategist, content ecosystem architect, SEO strategist, and digital product consultant. I will provide the sales copy of a product I want to sell online. Your job is to deeply analyze the sales copy and infer the **complete ecosystem around the type of people who would buy this product**. Your goal is NOT to summarize the product. Your goal is to extract the **full audience, problems, strategies, and opportunities** around this market. The typical users are **BEGINNER and INTERMEDIATE people who want practical strategies to grow in their activity domain.** STEP 1 — Analyze the sales copy and determine: • the activity domain • who the product is for • the type of activities these people perform • the broader ecosystem of their domain STEP 2 — Generate the following output. [1] Target Audience Audience consisting of beginner and intermediate [activity domain] users who want practical strategies to grow in their domain, including: • audience segment • audience segment • audience segment • audience segment • audience segment • audience segment • audience segment • audience segment • audience segment • audience segment • audience segment • audience segment • audience segment • audience segment • audience segment Generate **15–25 audience segments**. After completing this step, stop. Do NOT generate any additional sections yet. PRODUCT SALES COPY [Paste sales copy here]
Step 2: Topics Analysis
Continue the market ecosystem analysis using the same sales copy and reasoning from the previous step. Now generate the next section. [2] Topics For This Audience Topics relevant to their activity domain. Generate **30–50 topics** covering the full ecosystem. Include topics related to: • skills • tools • strategies • operations • marketing • growth • optimization • monetization • mindset • beginner guidance • scaling strategies Output only the bullet list. Stop after completing this section.
Step 3: Audience Pain Points
Continue the analysis using the same audience and domain identified earlier. Generate the next section. [3] Audience Pain Points Common struggles faced by this audience. Generate **20–30 realistic pain points** including: • beginner confusion • operational challenges • marketing difficulties • growth barriers • mindset struggles • technical problems Output only the bullet list.
Step 4: Content Ideas
Continue using the same audience and ecosystem. Generate the next section. [4] Content Ideas Generate **25–40 content ideas** that would attract this audience. Include ideas suitable for: • blog posts • YouTube videos • newsletters • social media posts • educational content Focus on **practical strategies and beginner guidance**.
Step 5: Lead Magnets
Continue using the same audience and ecosystem. Generate the next section. [5] Lead Magnet Ideas Generate **15–25 lead magnet ideas** that would attract this audience. Examples may include: • checklists • templates • guides • toolkits • cheat sheets • frameworks • mini-courses Each idea should be **practical and actionable**.
Step 6: Product Ideas
Continue the ecosystem analysis. Generate the next section. [6] Additional Product Ideas Suggest **15–25 digital product ideas** for this audience. Examples may include: • courses • templates • playbooks • toolkits • paid communities • workshops • SaaS tools Focus on products that solve real problems for this audience.
Step 7: Content Pillars
Continue the ecosystem analysis. Generate the next section. [7] Content Pillars Identify **5–7 major content pillars** for this audience. For each pillar include: Pillar: [name] Example topics: • topic • topic • topic • topic These pillars should represent the **core knowledge areas** of this domain.
Step 8: SEO Keyword Clusters
Continue the ecosystem analysis. Generate the next section. [8] SEO Keyword Clusters Generate **30–50 SEO-friendly topic ideas** suitable for search traffic. Examples: • how to [activity] for beginners • best tools for [activity] • beginner guide to [activity] • common mistakes in [activity] • step-by-step strategy for [activity] Focus on **high-intent search topics**.
Step 9: Monetization
Continue the ecosystem analysis. Generate the final section. [9] Monetization Opportunities Suggest **15–25 ways to monetize this audience**. Include opportunities such as: • digital products • courses • memberships • affiliate marketing • consulting • software tools • services • communities Focus on **realistic and scalable monetization models**. This is the final step.
